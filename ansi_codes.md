## Foreground (text) colors:

- Black: \033[30m
- Red: \033[31m
- Green: \033[32m
- Yellow: \033[33m
- Blue: \033[34m
- Magenta: \033[35m
- Cyan: \033[36m
- White: \033[37m
- Background colors:
---

## Text formatting options:

- Reset: \033[0m (resets all text formatting)
- Bold: \033[1m
- Underline: \033[4m
- Blink: \033[5m
- Reverse: \033[7m
